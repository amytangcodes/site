Amy Tang is a front-end developer living and working in Toronto, Canada.

In just over two years I discovered that I have a passion for creating beautiful and organized code. I enjoy working with HTML5, CSS3/Sass, Responsive Design, and ReactJS. I hunger to learn more about best coding practices and I want to work with people who are equally anal about code standards. As a developer at Info-Tech Research Group I work equally with the design team and the development team, being able to build both design and development skills.

Tools I've worked with include:

  • HTML5 / CSS3 80%
  • JavaScript 20%
  • Node.js 50%
  • Terminal | Github | SourceTree 70%
  • Ruby on Rails 20%
  • Handlebars | ReactJS 20%
